Welcome to Hoth Summers

Embark on two unique journeys across Hoth’s wild terrain, each offering an unforgettable adventure tailored to the brave at heart. Whether you seek the serenity of a thawed-out town where the warmth of summer brings life to the tundra, or you crave the untamed thrill of camping under the open sky amidst the presence of elusive Wampas, Hoth promises an experience unlike any other. Choose your path carefully, as these starkly different adventures are only for those who are ready to embrace the challenge.

Experience One

Welcome to Hoth, a once-frozen tundra that awakens each summer into a breathtaking oasis of life. Nestled between towering ice-capped peaks and vast, thawed-out valleys, this secluded town transforms from an icy wasteland into a stunning landscape of crystal-clear rivers, lush alpine meadows, and endless daylight. As the permafrost retreats, the town comes alive with vibrant flora, migrating wildlife, and an air of festivity as locals celebrate the warmth while it lasts. Whether you're here to explore the pristine wilderness or simply bask in the fleeting warmth, one thing is certain—your stay in Hoth is guaranteed to be a Wampa-free experience.

Experience Two

Welcome to Hoth’s Wild Expanse, an untamed stretch of tundra where only the bold dare to camp beneath the endless sky. As the summer sun melts away the harshest of winter’s grip, this remote wilderness offers breathtaking views of towering ice cliffs, shimmering glacial lakes, and the raw beauty of an open tundra untouched by civilization. But be warned—this is no ordinary camping trip. Wampas still roam these lands, their presence a constant reminder that only seasoned travelers should venture into the wild. If you crave adventure, solitude, and the thrill of the unknown, Hoth’s Wild Expanse awaits. Survival skills required.
